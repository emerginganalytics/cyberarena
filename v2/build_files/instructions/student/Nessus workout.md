# Nessus Workout Step-by-Step Instructions for Students: Getting Started with Logging In and Running Scans

1. **Logging In to Your Computer:**
   - Use the provided login details to access the Guacamole web server.
   - If the screen doesn't appear, try refreshing the page.
   - Once logged in, you'll be automatically directed.

2. **Accessing Nessus:**
   - All further steps will happen within a virtual machine.
   - Open Internet Explorer within the virtual machine.
   - Go to this web address: [https://10.1.1.27:8834](https://10.1.1.27:8834) (make sure it's "https" not "http").
   - You might see a warning. Click "More Information," then "Continue to this Site."
   - It might take a couple of minutes for the Nessus service to start.
   - On the login screen, use "cybergym" for both the username and password.

3. **Starting a Scan:**
   - Close any popup windows after logging in.
   - Click the top-right button labeled "New Scan."
   - Choose "Basic Network Scan" from the options.
   - You'll be taken to a form with three tabs: Settings, Credentials, and Plugins.

4. **Settings Tab:**
   - Give your scan a name.
   - In the "Target" box, type "10.1.1.27."

5. **Credentials Tab:**
   - Within the same form, go to the "Credentials" tab.
   - Choose "Windows" and enter "cybergym" for both the username and password.

6. **Saving Your Settings:**
   - Click "Save" after filling out the required fields.

7. **Launching the Scan:**
   - Your created scan will now show up under "My Scans."
   - Select the scan and click "Launch" at the top-right.
   - The scan will take around 10 minutes to complete.

8. **Viewing Scan Results:**
   - Once the scan is done, click on it to see the results.
   - You'll find five tabs: Hosts, Vulnerabilities, Remediations, Notes, and History.

9. **Completing Assessment Questions:**
   - You're now ready to answer the assessment questions on your Cybergym landing page.

Remember, if you encounter any difficulties, don't hesitate to ask for help.
